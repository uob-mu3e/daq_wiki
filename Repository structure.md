## Directories

- `common/`
    - `firmware/`
        - `assignments/` (pin assignments for different boards)
        - `s4/`
            - `ip/` (Stratix IV IPs)
            - xcvr, etc.
            - `include.qip`
        - `a5/`
            - `ip/` (Arria V IPs)
            - xcvr, etc.
        - `a10/`
            - `ip/`
            - xcvr, hex2seg7, etc.
        - `swb/` (switching board components)
        - `feb/` (FEB components)
        - `util/` (components that do not depends on board or subsystem)
      - libmudaq
- switching_pc
- fe_board
